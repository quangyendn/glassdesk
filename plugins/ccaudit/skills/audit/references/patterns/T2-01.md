# T2-01 — CLAUDE.md content quality

## Why it matters
Vague meta-instructions ("remember to", "always remember") are noise. They inflate CLAUDE.md without providing actionable guidance and signal the file needs a quality pass.

## Detection
Grep for low-quality instruction keywords across both user and project CLAUDE.md.

## Target
0-2 matches. Replace vague reminders with specific, imperative directives.
